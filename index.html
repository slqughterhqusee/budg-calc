Need help budgeting?


The traditional 50/30/20 budgeting strategy distributes money towards needs, wants, and savings. However, sometimes life isn't cut into such specific categories. Many needs and wants overlap; living an enriching life is important. This budget calculator will replace 'wants' with 'life', helping to shift your view. Instead of cutting fun things out because they're 'less important', think about what contributes to the cost of them!

If you don't have a job, but you want to see how life might look like with one, feel free to pick from the list! Behind the scenes, we first determine your monthly income. Next, we deduct 32.65% in taxes (an estimate determined by the EECU). Fun fact: Using the percent strategy, your maximum housing payment should be only 33% of your post-tax monthly income!
